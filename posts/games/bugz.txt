Bugz Bows and Curses is a procedurally generated rougelike revolving around completing Collections. You play as an Ant-ipede with a bow, as you go through levels you gain buffs and get stronger, but the stronger you are, the more likely the AI is to run.

%head: features
Procedurally generated maps:
Never see the same map twice,

Believable Ai with a fight or flight reflex:
AI that seems to think and react with reasonable actions. as these maps are generated In a fraction of a second every time you play.
